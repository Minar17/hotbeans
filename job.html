Responsibilities – Web Developer

By being a Web Developer for Hotbeans, you will have many responsibilities linked to your authority. This is to ensure our high standards are met and everything runs super smoothly. Listed below are a few responsibilities linked to you.

-Designing, building, testing and possibly maintaining a website or software application.

-Working with teams to resolve any conflicts, develop content criteria, prioritize requirements and find and agree o solutions.

-Performing and directing any website updates.

-Consistently backing up files.

-Making sure codes are met with company standard.
